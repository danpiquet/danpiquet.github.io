Lasercraft

I love designing for and programming CNC routers and lasers.

I use a Laguna SmartShop EX 150 watt CO2 laser. It's capable of cutting wood up to 1" thick.

Design

I primarily use Adobe Illustrator for the design portion of my process. Illustrator is an excellent program for the creation of scalable vector graphics that are easy to export into a readable format for CNC programming.

Setup

The next step involves setting up and programming the laser using a program called Lightburn.

Laser Cutting

Now for the fun part...

My CO2 laser is capable of cutting through wood up to 1" thick. Normally, however, I cut through quarter-inch plywood at about 20mm per second.

Assembly

I prefer to work with light-colored, raw-wood finishes, so all that is left to do is assemble and glue, then deliver to the customer.
